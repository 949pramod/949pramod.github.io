Industry automation using ARM processor

  • Tech Stack: : Embedded C, Embedded System, LPC1768 development Board

In the realm of industrial automation, this project harnesses the capabilities of the ARM Cortex-M microcontroller, specifically the LPC1768 model. The LPC1768, renowned for its robust performance and versatility, serves as the backbone of our system, enabling seamless integration and control of various automation features.

This project entails the incorporation of five pivotal features into industrial processes, all managed by the ARM Cortex-M LPC1768. Firstly, gas leakage is monitored using advanced sensors, ensuring swift detection and timely alerts to prevent potential hazards. Temperature sensors are employed to maintain a safe environment, triggering alerts through a buzzer if temperature thresholds are exceeded. The LPC1768 orchestrates automatic light control through an RTC, optimizing energy usage and maintaining optimal illumination. Additionally, smart door control ensures seamless operations, while an emergency shutdown feature adds an extra layer of safety. This amalgamation of functionalities is designed to augment industrial automation, relying on the LPC1768's computational capabilities to create a responsive and intelligent system

By implementing these ARM Cortex-M LPC1768-driven features, we strive to reduce manual intervention, enhance safety protocols, and streamline processes. Intelligent automation not only improves efficiency but also serves as a crucial step towards creating a safer industrial environment. As industries evolve, the integration of such advanced technologies becomes imperative for sustained growth, resilience, and, most importantly, the well-being of personnel and assets.
